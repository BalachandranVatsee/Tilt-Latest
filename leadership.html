Joseph(Joe)
George

Group Chairman & CEO

After more than 26 uninterrupted years, Joe resigned from Lintas as the Group Chairman & CEO for India, the Regional President for South & Southeast Asia and a Global Management Committee member of the MullenLowe Group network. Double hatting business and strategic planning roles right through, Joe was particularly very closely involved on the Unilever business with local, regional and global remits.

Joe founded Tilt with the vision of contemporarising the way brand and communication agencies are skilled, structured and outputting - for the changing consumer, the changing marketplace and the changing needs of marketers today.

Hari
Krishnan

Group Chief Business Officer

With stints across Lowe Lintas, Grey, JWT, Star TV and Mullen Lintas, Hari has over 25 years of experience – six of which were as CEO at MullenLowe Group, Sri Lanka and at Mullen Lintas, India. Hari’s experience straddles Automotive, BFSI, Consumer Durables, ECommerce, Fashion, Fintech, Foodtech, FMCG, Retail, etc. across brands like 3M, Akzo Nobel, Audi, Britannia, Flipkart, Ford, Future Generali, Havells, ITC Foods, PhonePe, Maruti Suzuki, Swiggy, Tanishq, Tata Tea, Titan and Unilever.

Hari is a firm believer that when it comes to brand building, creativity has only one purpose, which is, “mental availability”.

Michelle
Suradkar

Group Chief Operating Officer

Over more than 2 decades, Michelle has led organization and culture building and business-led HR practices – starting with the Coca Cola Company, and then in the Mullen Lowe Lintas Group, where she headed the HR function for over 10 years as the Group Chief Human Resources Officer and a member of the Managing Committee.

Michelle has an MBA from Bajaj and is a certified POSH trainer with a particular focus on DEI, and is also a certified Extended DISC administrator.

Gulshan
